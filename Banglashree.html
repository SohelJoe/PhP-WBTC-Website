Banglashree Services

All the District Head Quarters towns of the state are not presently connected with Kolkata by rail nor are there adequate modern AC bus services linking state Capital with district towns. There was a long cherished aspiration among the people that the district towns be connected with Kolkata by high speed modern AC VOLVO bus services so that they can travel overnight to reach Kolkata and return back conveniently. With the introduction of ‘Banglashree Express’ by the Hon’ble Chief Minister today the 18th July, 2018 the dream of millions has become a reality. Besides as part of ‘Safe Drive Save Life’ programme, Hon’ble Chief Minister also inaugurated 22 nos Taruma Care A.C. Ambulance with advance life support equipments and 13 nos Mechanical Wrecker Van. These ambulances and wrecker Vans were handed over to police, municipal and other local authorities for the service of people.
