Special Services

International Kolkata Book Fair happens to be the largest book fair in the world and a paradise for the book lovers. The identity of this Book Fair is now synonymous with the pride of Kolkata. The venue of the International Kolkata Book Fair has been shifted to Central Park, Bidhannagar due to ongoing renovation works at Milan Mela ground. Alike previous years, the Transport Department, Govt. of West Bengal, is privileged to be partner to the KIBF and committed to operate dedicated bus services for the visitors for their hassel-free and comfortable journey. The Transport Department, Govt. of West Bengal, has an exhaustive bus transport service for all booklovers of the city, suburbs and other districts. The South Bengal State Transport Corporation, along with the West Bengal Transport Corporation, will cater to the requirements of visitors for the first time. The Transport Department, Govt. of West Bengal, is ready to serve you all. ##[ THIS PAGE IS CURRENTLY UNDER MAINTAINENCE ]##
